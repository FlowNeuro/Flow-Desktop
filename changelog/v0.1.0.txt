FLOW DESKTOP CHANGE LOG
VERSION: 0.1.0
DATE: 2026-07-16
STATUS: PRE-RELEASE

ABOUT
Flow Desktop is a privacy-first YouTube and YouTube Music client - no account, no ads, no analytics, no telemetry. It is the desktop companion to Flow for Android.

WHAT IT DOES
- Watch YouTube videos and Shorts with native playback (no embedded web player)
- Browse a full YouTube Music experience: home, explore, charts, artists, albums and lyrics
- Download videos and music for offline use with a built-in muxer (no ffmpeg required)
- Skip sponsors and intros automatically via SponsorBlock, DeArrow and Return YouTube Dislike
- Get on-device recommendations from two local engines: FlowNeuro (video) and MusicBrain (music)
- Sync your library, likes and history with Flow for Android over your local network (peer-to-peer, no cloud)
- Follow channels, receive new-upload notifications, and manage playlists and watch history
- Update automatically through the built-in, signature-verified updater

SUPPORTED DEVICES
- Windows 10/11 (x64 and ARM64)
- macOS 13+ (Intel and Apple Silicon)
- Linux (x64 and ARM64 - AppImage, .deb, .rpm)
